About

CRB(Cheems Rock Band) is a self made club, formed by a group of students in National Institute Of Technology, Hamirpur. This band makes fun vidoes on latest trends , to make someone smile while watching us to get rid of all the depressions caused due to academics all.

How it started

It all started in a short vacartion after the end-sem exams. Some of us decided not to go home during this break. They felt so boring feeling all alone in the campus. They Came up with an idea why not to do something interesting. So we made our first content that was Dard bhari daastaan.
